What is Complimap?

Complimap is an experiment designed to see how far positivity travels. The concept is simple, get a compliment, mark it on the map, and pass the compliment on to someone else.

How does Complimap work?

1. Receive a compliment

If you're reading this, odds are you found a compliment code. That means someone chose to share some positivity with you!

That's pretty awesome!

If you haven't received a code yet, keep an eye out for a card with a code and a link to this page.

2. Add yourself to the map.

Once you've received a compliment code, click the red button in the upper right of the map above. Type in the code, your initials and an approximate location (country, state, or city would be best) and your location will be added to the map!

3. Keep the Positivity Going!

Once you enter your code, a new code will pop up! This code is for you to pass on to the next person in the chain. This can be anyone who you decide has done something good, kind, or just plain awesome!

Wonderful! How do I start?

Currently Compliments are spread individually, person-to-person. Once one comes your way, then you can add yourself to the map and then pass the positivity on to someone else.

If you are interested in starting your own chain, email me at complimap@wasv.me with what you want your compliment to be, your approximate location, and who you would give the compliment to. I'm going to slowly start adding compliment chains to the map, so it may take a while for me to get back to you.
